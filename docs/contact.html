Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about property inquiries and verification support

What’s the fastest way to reach you?

WhatsApp is the fastest (+254 704 564 880). We respond quickly during business hours. You can also call, email, or submit the form.

Do you assist with land verification?

Yes. We guide clients through verification steps like ownership checks, location confirmation, and transfer support — so you reduce risk before paying.

Do you work with diaspora clients?

Yes. We support Kenyans abroad with WhatsApp updates, video calls, and documentation guidance. We can coordinate site visits and keep you updated from start to finish.

Can I schedule a site visit?

Absolutely. Request a visit via WhatsApp, phone, or the form. We schedule visits based on availability, including weekends by arrangement.

What details should I share?

Property type, preferred location, budget range, timeline, and any verification needs (ownership, ground confirmation, transfer).

Is the consultation free?

Yes. Initial guidance is free. We focus on helping you make safe decisions and move forward confidently.
