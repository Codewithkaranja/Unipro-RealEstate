Due Diligence Checks (Before Any Payment)

We help reduce risk by confirming the right ownership and the right land records before you proceed.

  • Ownership certificate of search (lands registry)
  • Registry Index Map (RIM) confirmation
  • Identity confirmation of all parties
  • Capacity to transact (seller legitimacy)
Request Due Diligence Help

Legal Documentation (Agreement to Sale)

Through our partner advocates, we support drafting and execution of an agreement to sale and proper documentation flow.

  • Drafting the agreement to sale
  • Execution/signing guidance
  • Identity confirmation and legal compliance
  • Clean documentation trail for buyer protection

Ground & Survey Confirmation

We confirm location and size on the ground to ensure the plot you’re buying matches the records.

  • Location identification on ground
  • Area confirmation + report issuance
  • Boundary/beacon guidance (where applicable)
  • Site walk + buyer questions answered clearly

Subdivision & Land Control Board (LCB) Consent

Where required, we support subdivision processes and LCB consent applications to subdivide or transfer.

  • Subdivision of parcel (if necessary)
  • Land Control Board consent application
  • Coordination with planning/survey partners
  • Clear process timelines explained

Transfer Into Purchaser’s Name + Post-Transfer Search

  • Step-by-step guidance until completion
