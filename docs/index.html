Our Land Services

Land & plot support built around verification-first buying — ownership search, RIM checks, ground confirmation, advocate documentation, and transfer follow-through.

Land Verification & Due Diligence

We guide you through ownership searches, document checks, and risk red flags before you commit or send a deposit.

RIM, Map & Boundary Confirmation

We help confirm plot location using RIM/map checks and support boundary/beacon verification to reduce mismatch issues.

Site Visits & Ground Checks

Guided site visits to verify access roads, neighborhood, boundaries, and on-ground position — especially for first-time and diaspora buyers.

Advocate Support & Agreements

Trusted partner advocates assist with sale agreements, compliance steps, and ensuring the process is legally clean and documented.

Transfer Follow-Through

We support transfer into the buyer’s name and confirm completion using an official search certificate after registration.

Diaspora Land Buying Support

Remote assistance through WhatsApp updates, video calls, document sharing, and coordinated site visits — so you buy safely even while abroad.
